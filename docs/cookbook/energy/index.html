Smart Energy Meter

Introduction

Smart Energy Meter is a device which can be plugged to your electrical connection at you home and it will provide you with energy consumption, power usage and your energy bill in real-time.

Ingredients for the Recipe

  1. Raspberry Pi 3/4 (or any variant of these)
  2. SD card with Shunya OS installed, instructions
  3. Current sensor ACS712
  4. Analog to Digital Converter PCF8591
  5. OLED Display (size : 128x64) (optional)
  6. Wire Cutter
  7. Screwdriver
  8. Micro SD card Reader
  9. Female to Female Du-point cables

Conversion logic for Power and Energy

Power = Voltage x Current

AC voltages are fixed for a region, Check the AC voltage in your region and multiply it with current to get power.

Energy = Power / Time

Get time from the system and divide it by power to get energy consumption.
